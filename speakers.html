Prin Laomanutsak
"If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of giants." - Isaac Newton. To me, this is exactly what law is; discovery upon previous discoveries. I believe that a strong foundation of society is a robust legal system that bridge the gap between policy and implementation. I study law to understand the society and practice it to be empowered with the authority to, in essence, make the better society in my capability.
Chris Arscott
Chris Arscott was raised in the US and first moved to the Asia Pacific region in 1997 where he worked as a fluid mechanics engineer in Australia and Papua New Guinea. He traveled between Asia, the US, and Europe for several years before coming to reside in South East Asia in 2004. He holds a Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Stanford University and an MBA from INSEAD with supplemental studies at Harvard Business School, Dartmouth, UCLA, and USC.
